About This Course

Welcome to Advanced Cloud Native Architecture. This masterclass is designed for developers and architects who want to step beyond the basics and master the design, implementation, and deployment of resilient microservices architectures. We dive deep into real-world patterns, exploring everything from event-driven systems to service mesh implementation.

By the end of this course, you will be equipped with the practical knowledge to architect scalable platforms that can handle millions of users globally.

Design and deploy scalable microservice architectures using modern best practices.
